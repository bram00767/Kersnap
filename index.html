Kersnap

An online puzzle booklet by Bram Cohen

For each puzzle color every cell so that each color forms a single contiguous shape.
The shapes must be the same, but may be rotated or reflected.
Some puzzles have multiple solutions but no puzzle has two different solutions using different shapes.
